Email Extractor

An efficient tool to extract, validate, filter, sort, and export email addresses.

  • Extract Emails: Enter a block of text and extract email addresses.
  • Validation: Validate the extracted email addresses for correct formatting.
  • Remove Duplicates: Automatically remove duplicate email addresses.
  • Filtering: Filter emails based on specific domains or keywords.
  • Sorting: Sort emails alphabetically, by domain, or by length.
  • Export: Download the extracted emails in various formats.

To use the Email Extractor, simply enter your text in the input box and click the "Extract Emails" button. You can then apply filters, sort the emails, and export them as needed.
